About Me

Hi, I'm Ashwin. I am working currently as a Machine Learning Consultant at Sony India Software Centre, Bangalore, India on deputation from Ignitarium Technology Solutions, Pvt. Ltd, India. I spent a major part of my life in Dubai before relocating to India for pursuing Engineering. My journey toward professional excellence began with a Bachelor's degree in Engineering from Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala and a Master's degree in Computer Science with specialization in Machine Intelligence from the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIITM-K) and Management, Trivandrum, India. I completed my schooling from the Indian High School, Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

I am a TensorFlow Certified Developer (Tensorflow-Google) and have over 2.5 years of professional work experience working in AI related projects.

I'm deeply interested in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Autonomous Vehicles, Drones, Robotics, Product development and Management. Interested to devise and execute solutions for challenging tasks for a better tomorrow.
